## 2. The Narrative Arc (The "Why" of Jflow)

### The Origin Wound (1973–1983)

- **Environment:** 3rd Generation Jehovah's Witness. A high-control group characterized by "conditional love" and end-times fear
- **Family Dynamics:** Father was passive and emotionally distant. Mother was dominant and physically abusive, utilizing severe corporal punishment (coat hangers, vacuum cords)
- **Early Neurodivergence:** Often bored at Kingdom Hall, requiring frequent breaks. Felt fundamentally "different" from peers
- **Hidden Trauma:** Suffered sexual molestation by an older man and an older child—secrets buried for over thirty years
- **The Turning Point (Age 10, 1983):** The defining trauma. Unintentionally walked out of Kmart with a digital stopwatch. Mother reported him to Elders. He was **publicly reproved and shunned**
- **Impact:** At age 10, his entire social world treated him as invisible. This installed a core program of "I am inherently bad" and a massive fear of rejection. _"I had lost everyone in life that I had trusted."_
- **Institutionalization (Ages 12-14, 1985-1987):** Placed in mental hospitals twice for 6-8 weeks each. Put on Ritalin. Home life deteriorated into war zone—frequently locked out of house, once slept under neighbor's porch during storm

### The Wilderness Years (1988–2019)

- **The Exile (Age 15, 1988):** Kicked out of home permanently. Became "street kid" couch-surfing and surviving on fringes
- **Gang Phase:** Joined gang, adopted moniker **"Beo"**. Engaged in petty crime
- **Addiction:** Fell into drug use (crack, meth) to cope with rejection pain
- **Grandfather's "Tough Love" (1991):** Taken in by grandfather (an Elder), who later turned him in for credit card theft. Sent to jail, then rehab
- **The Mask:** Developed "Salesman Persona" using JW public speaking/door-to-door skills. Mastered sales (perfume, satellite TV, Crown super cleaner) despite being fired from nearly every wage job due to neurodivergent friction with authority
- **The Search for Connection:**
  - **First Marriage (1992, Age 19):** Impulsive marriage lasting 8 months after rehab
  - **Second Marriage (1993-1996):** Two children born, including son **Hunter** (1997). Divorced after 3 years
  - **Jasmine (2000, Age 27):** Third significant partner. Started private label cleaner business
  - **Priscilla (2016, Age 43):** Son born. She moved to California with child; they lost contact
  - **Beth (2017):** Encouraged him to get electrical contractor license
- **Grateful Dead Community:** Found temporary acceptance in hippie lifestyle/"Live to Give" ethos—first taste of unconditional community

### The Collapse & Rebirth (2019–Present)

- **Hurricane Florence (2018, Age 45):** Destroyed home. Moved into motorhome—start of nomadic, isolated RV lifestyle
- **Father's Death (2019, Age 46):** Father died of cancer. Tim began processing grief using psychedelics (**"The Orb experience"**)
- **The Pandemic (2020, Age 47):** COVID isolation in motorhome. Sold Ocean Electric business. Mental health spiral accelerated
- **Loss of License (2021, Age 48):** Lost electrical license due to non-response to board (executive dysfunction). Living off savings
- **Mother's Death (2022, Age 49):** Mother died. **Re-shunned at funeral** for admitting he was no longer believer. Unexpectedly inherited estate. Moved to **White Lake, NC**
- **Financial Collapse:** Burned through $500k savings during 2020-2023 isolation period
- **Total Isolation (2023, Age 50):** Severe agoraphobia sets in. Isolated for entire year—"rotting away"
- **The Crisis Call (Feb 2024, Age 50):** Life-saving call to crisis line. Received accurate diagnoses
- **The Interview (Nov 2024, Age 51):** Recorded comprehensive interview with **EXJW Diaries** on YouTube—breaking the silence
